### PT-BR TECHNICAL GLOSSARY:
1. ENFORCEMENT: Use **"Aplicação de Políticas"** or **"Imposição"**. NEVER use "Enforcamento".
2. THROTTLING: Use **"Throttling térmico"** (Brazilian devs keep the English term 'Throttling').
3. LIGHTWEIGHT: Use **"Leve"** or **"Baixo consumo de recursos"**. 
4. DASHBOARD: Keep as **"Dashboard"** or use **"Painel"**.
5. AGNOSTIC: Use **"Independente de hardware"** or **"Agnóstico"**.
6. FOOTPRINT: Use **"Consumo"** or **"Pegada de recursos"**.
